  2. DC, PBKS, SRH, KKR's another Failure

    3. It was again a heartbreak for the fans of DC, PBKS, SRH and KKR as the teams,like PBKS and KKR even after giving a glimpse of hope to their fans these teams again failed to qualify for the playoffs, due to their inconsistencies in both batting and bowling departments. This was a forgettable season for both DC and SRH as they finished at the bottom of the table. The inavailabilities of some of their important players also proved to be a big problem for some of these teams. The hope is never over, as who knows, we might see some of these teams in the playoffs next time around.

    Standings:
    KKR: 7th Place
    PBKS: 8th Place
    DC: 9th Place
    SRH: 10th Place

  3. Season of the Most Hundreds

    4. We can say this season to be "The Season of the Hundreds" as we saw a big jump in the previous record of the most hundreds in a single ipl season from 8 to 12. The Most Important of these came from Shubman Gill who scored 3 centuries in 4 games at the business end of the tournament. Then, there was Virat Kohli who finished the season with 2 consecutive Hundreds. The Other Centurions included the likes of H. Brooks, V. Iyer, SKY, Y. Jaiswal, H.Klassen, P. Singh and C. Green. This rapid increase is a result of better pitches as well as better approach of the players.

  4. The Brilliant Opening Duos

    5. The season had some brilliant opening Duos including the likes of Virat-Faf, Butler-Jaiswal and Conway-Gaikwad. While these players set up records for their partnerships, some teams also struggled with choosing their openers like SRH, KKR and DC. We see that teams with better openers finished better at the points table. While the Virat-Faf Duo scored a record breaking 900+ runs in their partnerships, the Conway-Gaikwad duo was not much behind with 800+ runs in their partnerships.

  5. RR Lost Momentum after a strong start

    6. Even after a strong start as well as a strong team, the 2022 season runner-ups failed to even qualify in this season's playoffs. This was a result of their bad outings in the later half of the season, as well as the inconsistencies from their batters other than Jaiswal.
