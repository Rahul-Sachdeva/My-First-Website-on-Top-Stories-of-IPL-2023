  1. How did the big buys fare out

    2. IPL is known for its big-money throughout the world, being one of the richest leagues of the world, but spending big money on players doesn't get you what you want, does it? Let's see. Sam Curran, the most expensive player in IPL history did good, but not as much as was expected from him. Cameron Green, the Second most Expensive Player was good with the bat but not that much with the bowl. Nicholas Pooran, he did the Best in expensive player category as he delivered at crunch moments, playing many impactful knocks throughout the season. But, there was also a category who did not do well at all, which included the likes the Ben Stokes, Harry Brooks and Mayank Agarwal. So it's not the money but the player that wins you the matches.

  2. RCB vs LSG Controversy

  3. The Most Awaited Final and a Salute to the Fans

    4. This time's IPL final will be remembered not only for its thrilling last ball finish, but also for its 3 day-contest. The Final originally being scheduled for 28 May, was moved to play on Reserve day that was 29 May, even on 29th May the rain did come again to extend the match to the early hours of 30th May, which was really shocking. Despite all this, the fans, deserved a huge salute as even not having an extra holiday, not even having place to stay, Some fans even had to stay on railway stations, did come to support their most loved team, isn't it what you say pure love for the team as well as the players. So, here we salute all the fans who are the heart of this tournament.

  4. GT's Another Season of Brilliancies

    Gujarat Titans, probably the best team of this season, didn't disappoint their fans this season again with a commanding run throughout the season. With the Orange Cap Holder Shubman Gill, The Top 3 Wicket Takers of the tournament, a nice Captain, best finishers and an All-Rounder Rashid Khan, with his co-Afghani Spinner Noor Ahmed. The Team had everything to win the Title again. But it was just not their day as CSK handed them a last ball loss in the final. Despite losing the Final, the Team did win hearts, and would be one of the favourites to win the title next time around.

  5. CSK and Thala Roar Again with their 5th Title

    Probably the most loved team of the IPL, CSK despite having a bad season the last time around, did comeback and comeback strong winning their 5th Title in the Captaincy of their Thala, Captain Cool, MS Dhoni. Mahi led the team to this comeback not only with his captaincy but also with his superb striking throught the season except the final. It was not only Dhoni, but a Supporting effort from each of the players, whether it was the Conway-Gaikwad opening Duo, Shivam Dube's Sixes, Rahane's Comeback, Rayudu's Final Innings, Jaddu's finale finish, Ali's All Round abilities, Pathirana's Yorkers, Theekshana's Carrom Balls, Deshpande's Wickets and Chahar's new ball Wickets. The Team was supported by their ever loving fans, who made look every ground as their home ground. Coupled with all these was their 5th Title which came at a thrilling content and after a wait 48 hours from what was scheduled. This made CSK the most successful team of the ipl alongside MI with 5 Title. The fans would be waiting to see if MSD plays another season for them.
